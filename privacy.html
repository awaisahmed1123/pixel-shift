Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Our Priority.

Pixel Shift is designed to be 100% private. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or the images you process. All operations, including converting, compressing, resizing, and watermarking, are performed directly in your web browser on your own computer.

Your files are never uploaded to any server. They remain on your device at all times. We believe in providing powerful tools without compromising your privacy.
