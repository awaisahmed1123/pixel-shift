About Pixel Shift

Our Mission

Welcome to Pixel Shift! Our mission is to provide a powerful, fast, and completely private suite of image processing tools accessible to everyone, directly within their web browser. We believe that professional-grade image manipulation shouldn't require expensive software installations or compromise your data privacy.

Privacy First

In an era where data privacy is crucial, Pixel Shift stands apart. We designed this tool with a "privacy-first" approach. Every operation – converting, compressing, resizing, filtering, and watermarking – happens entirely on your device. Your images are never uploaded to any server. What you process stays with you.

Key Features

Pixel Shift combines essential image utilities into one streamlined interface:

  • Batch Processing: Handle up to 500 images simultaneously.
  • Format Conversion: Easily switch between JPG, PNG, and WEBP.
  • High-Quality Compression: Reduce file sizes significantly while maintaining visual quality.
  • Precise Resizing: Define exact dimensions in pixels.
  • Creative Filters: Apply various filters to enhance your images.
  • Custom Watermarking: Protect your work with text or logo watermarks, including font choices, opacity, size, and position controls.

Built with Passion

Pixel Shift was developed by Awais Ahmed with a focus on clean design, efficient performance, and robust functionality. It leverages modern web technologies to deliver a desktop-like experience in your browser.
